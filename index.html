Winning Wednesdays Career Fair

You do not want to miss this job fair! Come dressed for success for an upcoming job fair being hosted at Laney College. The job fair is a partnership of Laney College, Oakland Public Library, Oakland Housing Authority, Alameda County Social Services, and the California Department of Rehabilitation.

FREE for all job seekers and employers!

*Open to All Candidates*

Where Is It?

The job fair is located at Laney College in the Student Center. The Student Center is just off the Quad in the middle of the campus. There is both metered and free street parking all around the Laney College campus, as well as several paid parking lots in downtown Oakland.

We encourage public transportation or other alternative modes of transportation. Laney College is located one block from the Lake Merritt BART Station. AC Transit bus lines 362, 88, 62, 59, 40, 11, 1, stop within walking distance of Laney College.
